Frequently asked questions.

What are credits?

CrowdHeavy Credits can be earned by Testers by submitting videos to tests and having them accepted. Crowdheavy pays testers $5 USD per credit earned on the first of every month. You will need a valid Cash App account that is eligible to receive payments.

Can I be paid via Paypal?

No. As of today, Cash App is the only for testers to be paid. We do not plan to support Paypal any time soon. However, more options are on the way!

How often are Testers paid?

Testers are paid on the first day of every month via Cash App.

What devices can I test with?

As of today, CrowdHeavy only supports web sites. We are hard at work to support more platforms as quikly as possible. Native iPhone, iPad, Android support is the works!
